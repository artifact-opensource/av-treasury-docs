Ecosystem Glossary Reference

Foundational terminology of the decentralized operational system matrix.

AMO (Autonomous Market Operations)

Sovereign smart contracts deployed directly to execute trading actions and maintain asset balances with zero dependency on human execution.

Artifact Utility (Au)

Ecosystem transactional reserve-backed token maintaining low-cost settlement properties with systematic transactional deflation rules.

Artifact Governance (Ag)

Structural delegation ledger tokens giving voter weight across parameters and execution paths based directly on active deposits.

PID Controller Engine

A mathematical algorithm tracking historical deviations between actual variables and targeted states, correcting drift continuously.

Total Value Locked (TVL)

The physical capital weight active across structural deposit contracts, denoting platform backing strength and asset utilization thresholds.

Slipstream Concentrated Positions

Targeted range-locked parameters for asset deposits in liquidity providers, ensuring optimization of systemic fee yields.
