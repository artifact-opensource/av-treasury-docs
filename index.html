08

Glossary

Every term in the ecosystem, defined.

Au (Artifact Gold)
Reserve-backed token. Each Au is backed by Treasury assets. Redeemable, stable, governance-controlled.
Ag (Artifact Silver)
Emission/reward token. Minted by PID, distributed to stakers. Drives demand and growth.
Treasury
Central vault holding all protocol reserves. Backing for Au tokens.
TreasuryAMO
Automated Market Operations. Deposits, withdrawals, liquidity via Slipstream.
PID Controller
Proportional-Integral-Derivative. Computes Ag emissions from TVL gap. The policy engine.
Governor
DAO governance contract. Propose, vote, execute. Includes timelock.
Timelock
Delay between proposal approval and execution. Ensures transparency.
AvOracle v5
Multi-source price feed with manipulation resistance. Provides Au/USD pricing.
RSBT
Rebasing Soulbound Token. Represents staked Au. Non-transferable. Rebases for rewards.
Epoch
Fixed time period for PID emission calculations. Controller distributes Ag once per epoch.
Slipstream
Concentrated liquidity AMM on Base. Used by TreasuryAMO for swaps.
TVL
Total Value Locked. USD value of assets in the protocol. Primary PID input.
Flywheel
Self-reinforcing cycle: TVL → PID → Ag → APY → demand → liquidity → TVL.
Base
Layer 2 blockchain (Coinbase L2). All contracts deployed here. Low fees, fast finality.
