01

Overview

AV Treasury is a decentralized central banking system on Base (Ethereum L2). It manages a dual-token economy: Artifact Utility (Au) as the reserve-backed medium of exchange, and Artifact Governance (Ag) as the PID-minted incentive and voting token. The system operates autonomously — monetary policy is enforced by code, not committees.

04

Economic Flywheel

The self-reinforcing cycle that drives protocol growth.

1

TVL Grows

Deposits flow into the protocol. TreasuryAMO manages liquidity. Total Value Locked increases toward the PID target.

2

PID Detects Gap

The PID controller computes the difference between current TVL and target TVL. Error signal drives emission rate.

3

Ag Emitted

New Ag is minted and distributed to stakers. Higher emissions = higher staking APY.

4

Staking Demand Rises

Higher APY attracts capital. More Au deposited, more Ag staked. TVL increases further.

5

Flywheel Reinforces

Higher TVL narrows the gap. PID reduces emissions. System converges to equilibrium at target TVL.

05

PID Controller

Proportional-Integral-Derivative controller. Computes Ag emissions from the TVL gap.

Emission Formula
E(t) = Kp·e(t) + Ki·∫e(τ)dτ + Kd·de(t)/dt
Kp · e(t) Proportional Reacts to current gap. Larger gap = more emissions.
Ki · ∫e(τ)dτ Integral Accumulates past error. Eliminates steady-state offset.
Kd · de(t)/dt Derivative Predicts future error. Dampens oscillation.

09

Glossary

AMO
Autonomous Market Operations. Protocol-controlled liquidity management without human intervention.
Artifact Utility (Au)
Reserve-backed ERC-20 token with 9bps transfer tax. Medium of exchange in the AV ecosystem.
Governance ERC-20 token. PID-minted, 100M cap. Grants voting rights in GovernorContract.
Artifact Governance (Ag)
Governance ERC-20 token. PID-minted, 100M cap. Grants voting rights in GovernorContract.
PID Controller
Proportional-Integral-Derivative. Control system that computes Ag emissions based on TVL gap from target.
TVL
Total Value Locked. The aggregate value of all assets deposited in protocol contracts.
Epoch
Fixed time window (5 days) over which PID emissions are computed and distributed.
TWAP
Time-Weighted Average Price. Oracle manipulation resistance mechanism used by AvOracle v5.
Slipstream
Base-native concentrated liquidity AMM. TreasuryAMO uses it for reserve management.
RSBT
Soulbound Receipt Token. Non-transferable ERC-721 representing a staked position.
FlashBuy
Buyback mechanism. Purchases Au from market when price falls below oracle, using protocol reserves.
Timelock
48-hour delay between proposal approval and execution. Ensures transparency and exit rights.
Quorum
Minimum participation threshold. 4% of total Ag supply must vote for a proposal to pass.
Reserve Ratio
Minimum 60% of Au supply must be backed by protocol reserves at all times.
